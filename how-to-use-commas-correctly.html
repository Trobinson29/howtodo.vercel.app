How to use commas correctly. Use a comma to separate three or more words phrases or clauses written in a series. A comma is necessary before the last and example. Its job is straightforward it introduces a clause of purpose a clause beginning with because answers the question why there s usually no comma before because.

When to use a comma before or. Use a comma after a phrase or clause that precedes the subject of a sentence. Lacking brothers and sisters i was shy and clumsy in the give and take and push and pull of human interchange updike 1989.

For example in the sentence drivers who exceed the speed limit are reckless there are no commas because the clause who exceed the speed limit is essential to the meaning of the sentence. You can also use a comma with a shorter phrase when you want to emphasize it or add a pause for literary effect. They re not as final as periods.

Use a comma after every three numbers when a number is bigger than 999 1 000 000. Commas customarily indicate a brief pause. If a dependent clause ends the sentence however it no longer requires a comma.

He hit the ball dropped the bat and ran to first base you may have learned that the comma before the and is unnecessary which is fine if you re in control of things. Commas always follow these clauses at the start of a sentence. Because is a slightly different animal. Dates that use the day month year format need a comma between the day and the month.
